Exam Traps — Don't Get Fooled

High-frequency confusion points in SSC / RRB / TNPSC exams
🪤
131st Amendment ≠ Educational InstitutionsIt was about OBC reservation in local bodies — defeated in Rajya Sabha
🪤
B.R. Gavai = 52nd CJI, NOT 51stSanjiv Khanna was the preceding CJI — Gavai is 52nd
🪤
2025 = 79th Independence Day1947 + 78 years = 2025, but it's the 79th celebration. Formula: Year − 1947 + 1
🪤
2026 = 77th Republic Day26 Jan 1950 was 1st. Formula: 2026 − 1950 + 1 = 77
🪤
UCC: Only Goa (NOT Uttarakhand)Uttarakhand drafted but not fully implemented. Goa is the ONLY state with UCC
🪤
Anti-defection = 10th Schedule, NOT 11th11th Schedule = Panchayati Raj (73rd Amendment)
🪤
Electoral Bonds: SC declared unconstitutional Feb 2024Not a 2025 event — SBI submitted data in 2024 per SC order
🪤
NOTA does NOT win electionsNOTA (None Of The Above) introduced 2013 — it does NOT trigger fresh elections
🪤
CAA rules implemented 2024, NOT 2025Citizenship Act 1955 amended; rules notified March 2024; grants continued into 2025
🪤
Constitution: Adopted 26 Nov 1949; In force 26 Jan 1950Samvidhan Diwas = Nov 26. Republic Day = Jan 26. Different dates!
🪤
Railway Budget merged since 2017, NOT 2016Union Budget 2017-18 was the first merged budget under Arun Jaitley
🪤
Finance Commission = Constitutional body under Art 28016th FC constituted 2024; Dr Arvind Panagariya is Chairman
🪤
Rajya Sabha is NOT dissolved1/3 members retire every 2 years — it is a permanent house
🪤
Lok Adalat awards = FINAL and bindingNo appeal lies against Lok Adalat awards in any court
🪤
ADR = Association for Democratic Reforms (NOT a govt body)Independent non-profit that tracks electoral candidates' backgrounds
🪤
MPLAD = ₹5 crore/year/MPMembers of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme — enhanced to ₹5 crore
🪤
SC = 34 total judges including CJIIncreased from 31 by Parliament. Currently 32 of 34 posts filled (June 2026)
🪤
Padma Awards announced on Republic Day EVENOT on Republic Day itself — announced the evening of 25th January
🪤
Article 370 abrogation = August 2019; SC upheld = December 2023Not a 2025 event — don't confuse the year of abrogation vs SC verdict
🪤
CJI appointment = seniority convention, NOT politicalSenior-most SC judge becomes CJI — NOT a political appointment
🪤
Delimitation is AFTER CensusCensus postponed to 2026 → Women Reservation also delayed → 2029 cycle
🪤
GST launched 1 July 2017 — "One Nation One Tax"101st Constitutional Amendment. Not 2016, not 2018.

⚖️ Five Writs (Art 32 — SC | Art 226 — HC)
Habeas Corpus
"Produce the body"
Protects against illegal detention
Mandamus
"We command"
Forces public official to perform duty
Prohibition
"To forbid"
HC stops lower court proceedings
Certiorari
"To be certified"
Quash lower court order
Quo Warranto
"By what authority"
Challenge to a public appointment
⚠️ Exam Trap: Art 32 (SC writs) is a Fundamental Right itself — Art 226 (HC writs) is NOT a FR, it's a constitutional right.

Key Amendments — Visual Timeline

Most frequently asked in SSC CGL / RRB NTPC
42nd Amendment 1976
Added "Socialist" & "Secular" to Preamble; added Fundamental Duties (Art 51A) — called "Mini-Constitution"
44th Amendment 1978
Restored right to property as legal right (not fundamental) — Art 300A
52nd Amendment 1985
Anti-defection law — added 10th Schedule to Constitution
61st Amendment 1988
Voting age reduced from 21 → 18 years
73rd Amendment 1992
Panchayati Raj — added 11th Schedule. Mandatory elections, reservations, 5-year term
74th Amendment 1992
Urban Local Bodies (Municipalities) — added 12th Schedule
86th Amendment 2002
Right to Education — Art 21A (6-14 years free education)
101st Amendment 2016
GST — One Nation One Tax; came into force 1 July 2017
103rd Amendment 2019
10% EWS (Economically Weaker Sections) reservation

Exam Traps — Economy Edition

Most commonly confused in SSC CGL / RRB NTPC
🪤
Repo Rate ≠ Reverse Repo RateRepo = RBI lends to commercial banks. Reverse Repo = commercial banks park money with RBI (currently 5.50%)
🪤
CPI ≠ WPICPI = Consumer Price Index = retail inflation. WPI = Wholesale Price Index = wholesale level. RBI targets CPI, NOT WPI.
🪤
FDI ≠ FIIFDI = Foreign Direct Investment (long-term, company stake). FII = Foreign Institutional Investor (short-term, stock market).
🪤
Forex Rank: USA > China > Japan > India (4th)India is 4th largest forex reserve holder globally. Common trap — India is NOT 3rd.
🪤
Sensex = 30 stocks (BSE) ≠ Nifty = 50 stocks (NSE)BSE = Bombay Stock Exchange (Sensex). NSE = National Stock Exchange (Nifty 50). Different exchanges!
🪤
RBI MPC = 6 members: 3 RBI + 3 ExternalNot 4, not 5, not 8. Exactly 6. External members are appointed by the Government (NOT RBI).
🪤
NPA = 90+ days overdue (NOT 30 or 60)Non-Performing Asset = loan where repayment is overdue for more than 90 days.
🪤
IMF ≠ World Bank (both in Washington DC)Both HQ in Washington DC. IMF = monetary stability. World Bank = development lending. Different institutions!
🪤
RBI Governor = Sanjay Malhotra = 25th (NOT 26th)The November 2025 PDF had a typo. Verify carefully — Sanjay Malhotra is the 25th RBI Governor.
🪤
Aadhaar issued by UIDAI under IT Ministry (NOT RBI, NOT Finance Ministry)UIDAI = Unique Identification Authority of India. Under Ministry of Electronics and IT (MeitY).
🪤
₹12.75 lakh tax-free = ₹12L income + ₹75,000 standard deductionUnder new tax regime default. The ₹75,000 is standard deduction — it's NOT a rebate. Total effective tax-free = ₹12.75 lakh.
🪤
Jan Dhan = zero-balance basic savings accounts (not regular)PM Jan Dhan Yojana (2014) — basic savings bank account, no minimum balance required.
🪤
EPFO = Labour Ministry (NOT Finance Ministry)Employees' Provident Fund Organisation is under Ministry of Labour & Employment.
🪤
CEPA ≠ FTACEPA = Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (covers trade + investment + services). FTA = Free Trade Agreement (mainly goods).
🪤
Unicorn = startup valued $1 billion+ (NOT publicly listed)A unicorn is a private startup. Once it lists on a stock exchange, it is no longer called a unicorn. India has 113 (3rd globally).
🪤
D-SIBs = SBI, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank ("Too big to fail")Domestic Systemically Important Banks require higher capital buffers. Axis Bank is NOT a D-SIB.
🪤
GIFT City = Gujarat, NOT MumbaiGujarat International Finance Tec-City (GIFT City) is near Gandhinagar, Gujarat. India's first IFSC.
🪤
Mudra Loan Tiers: Shishu <₹50K · Kishore ₹50K–5L · Tarun ₹5L–10L · Tarun Plus ₹10L–20LAll four tiers asked in exams. Shishu = smallest. Tarun Plus added later for ₹10–20 lakh.
🪤
G7 ≠ G20 — India is G20, NOT G7G7 = USA, UK, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, Canada. India held G20 presidency in 2023. 2026 presidency is NOT India.
🪤
Udyam portal ≠ Udyog AadhaarUdyog Aadhaar was replaced by Udyam portal in 2020. MSME registration now happens on Udyam. 5 crore MSMEs registered.
🪤
Railway Budget merged since 2017 (Union Budget 2017-18)First budget to merge both was presented by Arun Jaitley on 1 Feb 2017. NOT 2016.
🪤
RBI established April 1, 1935 | Nationalised 1949 | HQ MumbaiSet up on recommendations of Hilton Young Commission. NOT a government department — it's a statutory body.
🪤
SEBI: Established 1988 (non-statutory) → 1992 (statutory by SEBI Act)Many confuse 1988 and 1992. SEBI got statutory powers only in 1992. HQ Mumbai.

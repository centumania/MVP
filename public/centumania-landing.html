Got questions?

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before joining.

What if I miss a day's exam?
▼
Missing a day means you miss that day's points and your streak resets. The daily exam window is strictly 6:00–8:30 AM IST — there are no makeup attempts. This is by design: the discipline is the product.
Which exam is this specifically for?
▼
CentuMania is specifically designed for the Puducherry LDC (Lower Division Clerk) and UDC (Upper Division Clerk) competitive exams. The question patterns, subject weightages, and syllabus coverage are all modelled on these specific exams.
What language are the exams in?
▼
Exams are in English and Tamil, matching the actual LDC/UDC exam format. All explanations and answer keys are provided in both languages.
How does the refund work?
▼
If you attempted every single exam without missing a day, maintained an average score below 50%, and submit a refund request within 7 days of programme completion — you get a full refund. Eligibility is verified automatically from your exam data.
Can I join mid-programme?
▼
No — cohorts start together on a fixed date. This is essential for the leaderboard and peer accountability to work. Join the waitlist to be notified when the next batch opens.
Is there a free trial?
▼
There is no free trial — but the refund guarantee means you can try the full programme risk-free. If you show up every day and still don't improve above 50%, you get your money back.
